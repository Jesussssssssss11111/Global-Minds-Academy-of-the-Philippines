About Global Mind's Academy of the Philippines

Global Mind's Academy of the Philippines (GMAP) is an educational institution offering programs from pre-school to Senior High School. Our enrollment is ongoing from April 1st to June 18th. We provide Free Tuition Fee for Senior High Voucher Grantees and Free Uniforms for all students.

VISION

We envision Global MInds Academy of the Philippines as an excellent educational institution with a global perspective- emphasizing educational quality, values, and leadership skills and relevant to personal, community and national development.

MISSION

The Global Minds Academy of the Philippines prepares and trains its students for a rewarding and dynamic life in which individual talents and skills are constantly developed, challenged and applied as part of their lifelong journey to learning.

Academic Strands

We offer the following academic strands: ICT, HUMSS, ABM, GAS, and HE.

College Courses

If you choose specific strands:


  • If you pick to become an ABM student the possible college courses you will be getting is, Bachelor of Science in Accountancy (BSA), Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA), Bachelor of Science in Management Accounting (BSMA), Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship, And Lastly the Bachelor of Science in Finance. Choose Accountacy and Business Management: Shape Business,Master Finance, Lead Tomorrow!

  • If you pick to become a HE student the possible college courses you will be getting is, Culinary Arts or Culinary Science, Nutrition and Dietetics, Hotel and Restaurant Management, Food Technology, and Lastly the Family and Consumer Sciences. Choose Home Economics: Craft,Cook,Design, And Nurture your Future!

  • If you pick to become a HUMSS student the possible college courses you will be getting is, Bachelor of Arts in Communication, Bachelor of Arts Psychology, Bachelor of Arts in Political Science, Bachelor of Science in Social Work,and Lastly the Bachelor of Arts in Sociology. Choose Humanities and Social Sciences: Explore MInds, Cultivate Perpectives, Shape Sociteties for a Brighter Tomorrow!

  • If you pick to become a GAS student the possible college courses you will be getting is, Bachelor of Science in Psychology, Bachelor of Science in Communication, Bachelor of Arts in Political Science, Bachelor of Science in Economics, And Lastly the Bachelor of Science in Education. Choose General Academic Stand: Explore, DIscover, Excel in a World of Oppotunities!

  • If you pick to become an ICT student th possible college courses you will be getting is, Bachelor of Scince in Information Technology, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering, Bachelor of Science in Digital Marketing, And Lastly the Bachelor of Multi-media Arts. Choose Information and Communications Technology: Innovate, Create, Transform for a Tech-Driven Future!
